PRIVACY POLICY

Kindly Note this disclaimer

Welcome to the Ghana Police MTTD official application named MTTD alert on road. This app seeks to educate the general public and road users on the correct and safe use of all types of roads in Ghana and what the Road Traffic Act, 2004 (Act 683) and the regulations stipulates through technology.

Our Privacy Policy

Ghana Police Service, Police MTTD of Republic of Ghana, collects information on how you use this application so that we can improve our software and deliver a better experience. To do this, we use unique device identifiers to identify your computer or mobile device. This application also logs information such as your operating system and IP address.

This application does not collect any personal information aside your phone number for verification purposes.

Ghana Police Service, Police MTTD of Republic of Ghana, does not allows advertising companies to collect data through our service for ad targeting. We do not share data collected by this application with other organizations for any purpose.

This privacy policy was last updated on 01/3/2020. Our privacy policy may change from time to time.If we make any material changes to our policies, we will place a prominent notice within the application.
